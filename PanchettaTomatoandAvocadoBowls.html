Panchetta, Tomato and Avocado Grain Bowls

These hearty breakfast grain bowls feature grilled avocado, poached eggs, candied pancetta, quinoa, watercress and cherry tomatoes for a blast of flavor. Packed with fiber and protein, this bowl will power you through your day.

Ingredients:

1 ½ tablespoons light brown sugar

½ teaspoon smoked paprika

¼ teaspoon ground fennel seeds

4 (1-oz.) uncured pancetta slices

1 cup quinoa

2 ripe avocados, halved

4 large eggs, poached

2 cups packed watercress

1 pt. cherry tomatoes, halved

4 teaspoons olive oil

½ teaspoon kosher salt

½ teaspoon black pepper

Directions:

STEP 1: Preheat oven to 300°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

STEP 2: Stir together brown sugar, paprika and fennel in a small bowl. Place pancetta on prepared baking sheet. Rub brown sugar mixture evenly on both sides of pancetta slices. Bake in preheated oven until crispy, 20 to 22 minutes.

STEP 3: Meanwhile, cook quinoa according to package directions. Set aside.

STEP 4: Heat a lightly greased cast-iron grill pan over medium-high. Place avocados, cut side down, on grill pan; grill until char marks appear, about 1 minute. Rotate each avocado 45° (to create crisscrossed marks), and grill until char marks appear, about 1 minute. Set aside.

STEP 5: Divide quinoa evenly among 4 shallow bowls. Top each with 1 grilled avocado half, 1 candied pancetta round, 1 poached egg, 1/2 cup watercress and some tomatoes. Drizzle the watercress, avocado and tomatoes with oil. Sprinkle the bowls with salt and pepper.

Nutrition Facts:

573 Calories

36g Fat

45g Carbs

20g Protein
